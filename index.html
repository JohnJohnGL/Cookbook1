My love of baking started when I moved to Europe and saw how Europeans celebrate birthdays. In the US, the person having a birthday prepares nothing.. other people do all the baking. In Europe, the person having a birthday does all the baking. I started with having to bake my own chocolate birthday cake and my colleagues said it was delicious. Baking has grown into a hobby that myself and others can enjoy at the same time.
- Jennifer
